Our Team
“UNITY IN PURPOSE, DIVERSITY IN TALENT; TOGETHER, WE SHAPE EXCELLENCE”

Meet the driving force behind Alight India Foundation, where dedication, compassion, and creativity converge to transform lives. At Alight India Foundation, our success is powered by a passionate and skilled team. Comprising educators, social workers, volunteers, and professionals with a shared commitment to uplifting communities, each member contributes their expertise to every initiative we undertake. Together, we embody the spirit of hope and innovation, ensuring that every Alight India Foundation project reflects our unwavering dedication to empowerment and positive change.

Message from our Chairman

Alight India Foundation was established in 2013 with a vision to elevate education and social welfare in India. Over the years, we've adapted and innovated our initiatives in line with the evolving needs of communities, positioning ourselves at the forefront of educational and social empowerment. We always keep the needs of those we serve at heart, striving to provide accessible and impactful solutions for a brighter future.

"The mission of Alight India Foundation is more than just providing education; it's about transforming lives, building brighter futures, and creating lasting change in our communities."
