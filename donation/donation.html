Thank you for supporting the Alight India Foundation

Childhood is often idealized as the most enjoyable phase of human lifeâ€”a time of carefree innocence and happiness. Unfortunately, this idealized experience is not universal, especially for children who are unable to attend school. Many factors contribute to children leaving school prematurely, ranging from difficult socio-economic conditions to communities where education is undervalued and awareness is lacking.

Attending school not only secures a promising future and joyful present for children but also provides a secure environment for self-expression, learning, sharing, and personal development. Children who are unable to continue their education often face the harsh realities of child labor, early marriage, or exploitation through trafficking.

The Alight India Foundation, through its 'Education Uninterrupted' initiative, is dedicated to supporting children facing challenging circumstances to pursue their education. This effort aims to empower them with the opportunity for a brighter future and improved quality of life. Currently, we are actively involved in providing educational support to more than 120,000 children across 27 states in India.

Despite facing numerous challenges, these resilient young champions have persevered and continue to dream big while working hard to achieve their goals. Your support can help us provide them with accessible and high-quality education, empowering them to pursue their dreams. Together, let's join hands to ensure every child enjoys a happy and secure childhood!
